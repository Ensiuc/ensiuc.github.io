About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher exploring the epigenetic regulation of oocytes and early embryonic development. My work focuses on DNA methylation (CpG and CpH contexts), single-cell methylome sequencing, and biomarker discovery for reproductive health. I'm passionate about translating cutting-edge epigenetic research into real-world biotech impact.

Research Projects

  • Single-Cell Methylation in Oocytes – Identified CpH and CpG patterns under stress. Published in Molecular Ecology.
  • Domestication Study in 1-Year Fish – Used PacBio HiFi sequencing to explore transgenerational epigenetic changes.
  • Chromatin State Mapping – Integrating methylation with chromatin state and motif discovery in early embryos.
